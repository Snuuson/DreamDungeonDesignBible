Appendix · shared terminology

Glossary

Core terms used throughout the Design Bible and spell reference. Spell descriptions link here where applicable.

Gameplay terms

Definitions

Auto attacks
Baseline attacks that remain relevant throughout a run. They can critically strike and complement abilities rather than acting as meaningless filler.
Conditions
Combat states such as Burning, Poisoned, Marked, Rooted, Immobilized, Bleeding, Silenced, and Weakened. They create clear interactions between spells.
Crowd control
Effects that limit an enemy’s actions or movement. Strong effects should generally need setup, interact with Weakened, and respect diminishing returns or immunity rules.
Health
The universal life resource. Reaching zero health defeats a unit.
Mana
The universal resource spent to use spells. Physical and melee spells may generally cost less mana than magical spells.
Momentum
An active combat rhythm for many offensive builds. It can enhance or extend selected spells but is not intended to be mandatory for every build.
Relics
Rare boss rewards intended to be spent at Altars to acquire Talents.
Shared XP
Experience earned by the party together. Level thresholds trigger spell drafts, allowing pacing to be tuned separately from encounter count.
Threat
The system governing enemy behavior. It gives tank-oriented builds a meaningful role without forcing every party into a fixed composition.
Weakened
A condition that enables or improves certain crowd-control effects and spell interactions.
