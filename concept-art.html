Concept Art

Every Act keeps its defining palette, lighting, materials, and depth in the journey while two connected Sections express different Color influences. These three rounds test directed, naturally compatible, and deliberately difficult pairings.

Round 1 · Directed pairings

The original Color study

The requested combinations establish how two distinct Color identities can coexist within one coherent Act and Dungeon stretch.

Round 2 · Best fit

Pairings that reinforce each Act

These combinations were selected because both Colors naturally strengthen the Act's materials, light, ecology, or narrative role.

Round 3 · Design challenge

Difficult pairings, made coherent

These combinations intentionally resist their Acts. Each reinterprets the Colors through local materials and mechanics so the Act remains dominant.
