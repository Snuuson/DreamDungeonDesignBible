Environment direction · Six Acts · Seven rounds

Color-Influenced Sections

Each Act keeps its defining palette, lighting, materials, and place in the journey while two connected Sections express different Color influences. Round 6 widens the encounter floors and introduces readable enemy groups; Round 7 adds hostile nonhuman humanoids to mixed creature factions. All concepts remain free of player characters and UI.
