Environment direction · Six Acts · Eleven rounds

Color-Influenced Sections

Each Act keeps its defining palette, lighting, materials, and place in the journey while connected Sections express different Color influences. Round 10 moves the camera onto the playable floor; Round 11 introduces a persistent party of four knights whose equipment grows from improvised Act 1 kit to relic-grade Act 6 armor. The concepts remain free of UI.
